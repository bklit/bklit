# Updating Billing

This guide covers managing your Bklit subscription and billing.

## Accessing Billing Settings

1. Go to your workspace dashboard
2. Click "Settings" in the sidebar
3. Click "Billing"

Or navigate directly to:
```
/{organizationId}/settings/billing
```

## Upgrading to Pro

### Step 1: Navigate to Billing

Go to workspace settings â†’ Billing

### Step 2: Click Upgrade

Click the "Upgrade to Pro" button

### Step 3: Complete Checkout

You'll be redirected to Polar checkout:
1. Review plan details
2. Enter payment information
3. Complete purchase

### Step 4: Confirmation

After successful checkout:
- Plan updates immediately
- Pro features are enabled
- Billing portal access is available

## Managing Subscription

### Access Billing Portal

1. Go to Billing settings
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Opens Polar billing portal

### Available Actions

In the billing portal, you can:
- Update payment method
- View invoices
- Download receipts
- Change billing period
- Cancel subscription

## Downgrading to Free

### Step 1: Cancel Subscription

1. Go to Billing settings
2. Click "Manage Subscription"
3. Cancel subscription in Polar portal

### Step 2: Plan Reverts

- Subscription remains active until end of period
- Plan reverts to Free at period end
- Pro features disabled after period ends

### Step 3: Data Retention

- All data is retained
- Access to historical data remains
- New limits apply (projects, events, etc.)

## Payment Methods

### Adding Payment Method

1. Go to Billing portal
2. Click "Payment Methods"
3. Add credit card or other method
4. Set as default

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to Billing portal
2. Click "Payment Methods"
3. Update existing method
4. Save changes

## Invoices and Receipts

### Viewing Invoices

1. Go to Billing portal
2. Click "Invoices"
3. View all past invoices
4. Download PDF

### Receipts

Receipts are automatically emailed after each payment.

## Subscription Status

### Active

- Subscription is active
- Pro features available
- Billing continues automatically

### Canceled

- Subscription canceled
- Access until end of period
- Reverts to Free after period

### Past Due

- Payment failed
- Pro features may be limited
- Update payment method to restore

## Billing Period

### Monthly

- Billed monthly
- Recurring charge each month
- Cancel anytime

### Yearly

- Billed annually
- Often includes discount
- Cancel anytime (prorated refund)

## Troubleshooting

### Payment Failed

1. Check payment method is valid
2. Update payment method if needed
3. Contact support if issue persists

### Subscription Not Updating

1. Check webhook is configured
2. Verify Polar webhook secret
3. Check webhook logs
4. Contact support if needed

### Wrong Plan

1. Verify subscription in Polar
2. Check webhook received events
3. Refresh dashboard
4. Contact support if needed
