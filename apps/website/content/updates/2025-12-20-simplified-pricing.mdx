I've simplified Bklit's pricing to make it easier to understand and more accessible for everyone. The new structure has just two tiers: Free and Pro.

## Free Plan

The free plan is perfect for side projects, personal sites, or testing Bklit:

- 4,000 events per month
- 1 project
- 1 team member
- All core analytics features
- API access
- Community support

This gives you plenty of room to track a small to medium-sized website without any cost.

## Pro Plan

For growing businesses and serious projects, the Pro plan offers:

- 100,000 events per month included
- $0.0004 per event after that (usage-based)
- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited team members
- Everything in Free, plus priority support

At $10/month base, it's designed to scale with you. The usage-based pricing means you only pay for what you actually use beyond the base allocation.
